# Blog Reference Design Notes — bhavleensingh.com/blog

## Layout
- Dark hero section (deep forest green ~#1a2e1a or similar dark bg) with white text
- "INSIGHTS & PERSPECTIVES" label in yellow-green accent colour, with a left vertical bar
- Large bold "The SEO Blog" heading in white
- Subtitle text in light grey/white below
- Filter bar below hero: "FILTER:" label + pill buttons (All, SEO Strategy, Technical SEO, Local SEO)
  - Active pill: filled yellow-green background with dark text
  - Inactive pills: outlined/border only
- 3-column card grid for articles
- Light/off-white background for the card grid section

## Cards
- Bordered cards with a dashed/dotted border style (light green dashes)
- Top row: category tag pill (small, filled, rounded) + read time with clock icon
- Bold article title (2-3 lines)
- Excerpt paragraph (3-4 lines)
- Bottom row: date with calendar icon (left) + "Read more →" link in accent colour (right)
- No images on cards — pure text cards

## Footer strip
- Dark background (same as hero)
- "Want SEO advice tailored to your business?" heading in white
- Subtitle in grey
- CTA button in accent colour

## Typography
- Serif or heavy sans-serif for headings
- Clean sans-serif for body

## Colours (reference site)
- Dark bg: deep forest green
- Accent: yellow-green / lime

## Khalis Brand Adaptation
- Dark bg: #04315b (navy)
- Accent: #ffdb14 (yellow)
- Cards: white bg with subtle border
- Active filter pill: #ffdb14 bg with #04315b text

## CHOSEN DIRECTION: Option C — Obsidian Glow

Deep obsidian dark background with emerald green accent (growth/SEO), bento grid layout, glowing card borders, cinematic scroll animations via Framer Motion. Typography: Syne for headings, Outfit for body text.
